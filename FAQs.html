FinoTrust - FAQs

1. What is the reason for FinoTrust's existence?

Founders of Finotrust believe consumers in India can be guided and educated much better when it comes to implementing a credible savings plan . For too long financial products have missold and consumers have been ill-advised by distributors, banks, agents, etc. and as a result large trust deficit has been created. Consumers don't know who to trust and who to turn to for honest advise that they can easily understand.
Finotrust aims to fill this trust deficit by engaging with consumer over the long term, by providing need and goal-based advise, and by providing transparency on everything it does. It is a long journey and Finotrust will gain confidence of each customer at a time.

2. What will exactly FinoTrust provide?

Finotrust aims to be the 'one-stop' for all your financial savings needs. You can get educated through our Knowledge Centre, select a plan or then modify a customized savings plan which is based on your risk tolerance levels and investment horizon. And most importantly, buy the recommendations of the plan in an easy and straightforward way. Over time you can conveniently track your financial progress towards your long term or even short term financial goals.

3. What aspects of Savings & Investments will FinoTrust advice on?

FinoTrust believes financial savings incorporates all financial products including Fixed Deposit (FD), Mutual Funds, Public Provident Fund (PPF), National Pension Scheme (NPS), Li. Finotrust believes individual consumers should not picking single stocks and that should be left to professional managers. Going forward we will also integrate Life and Non-Life Insurance in our offerings.

4. How is FinoTrust different from other online websites, distributors, agents, etc.?

  • FinoTrust is only available platform online to manage all your's and your family saving and investment needs. We also monitor your investment portfolio closely.

  • Our advice is customized for your financial needs and enabling you to achieve your goals. Unlike traditional services, we will advise you on your overall portfolio vs. your financial goal, irrespective of the amount you have invested.

  • Transparency on all aspects of the business including the commission structures of the financial product (including if any commission paid to FinoTrust). Although there are many players who provide sound financial advise many often misquote or avoid discussions around suitability and commission structure.

  • Provide real world and relevant examples for you to relate and benchmark to folks in similar shoes and how they are saving for their future needs.

5. Why should I trust FinoTrust advice? Aren't you just another website trying to create a business for yourself.

  • Unlike other players in the industry, we aren't a black box and we can provide you insights why chose a certain plan or product for you. We realize it is your money and you need be comfortable with it the most.

  • Yes we are a for-profit business and we have to sustain and grow our operations. But we are in this for the long term as we strongly believe short term selling pressure has led to some of the trust deficit problems in the industry today.

  • Finotrust founders have a collective experience of 45+ years in the financial industry who understand the nuances of most financial products. We have also seen the up and down swings in markets and economy and this prepares us well to guide consumers on well devised savings plans

  • Use technology to systematically evaluate available financial products and appropriately match it to your financial needs. However, realize machines and technology aren't perfect so FinoTrust gives you the flexibility to modify our recommendations.

  • Last but not least, Finotrust engages with industry experts and tax experts in evaluating financial products and developing financial savings plans.
